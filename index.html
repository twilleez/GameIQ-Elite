Terms & Privacy

Last updated 2025 · All data stored locally on your device only.

Terms of Service

GameIQ Elite is provided for personal coaching and statistical tracking. All data you enter is stored locally in your browser. We do not collect, transmit, or sell your data. The app is provided "as is" without warranty. We are not liable for data loss — use Export Backup regularly.

Privacy Policy

No personal data is sent to any server. No analytics, tracking, or cookies beyond localStorage are used. The only external network request is Google Fonts loading. AI Coach feature sends your anonymized game context to Anthropic's API when used. Player names are never sent — only aggregate stats. You can delete all data via Settings → Clear All Data at any time.

Children's Privacy

If tracking minors, use jersey numbers or initials rather than full names. Ensure you have appropriate consent from parents or guardians before tracking personal information about players under 18.
