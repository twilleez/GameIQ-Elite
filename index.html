Welcome to GameIQ Elite 👋

The sideline command center built for real coaches. Here's a quick walkthrough to get you up and running.

1
Start with the Dashboard
The coach dashboard gives you live reads, AI insights, and team context before you jump into tracking.
2
Add your roster
Go to Live Game, add players with jersey numbers. Your roster saves automatically between sessions.
3
Quick Game Mode for the sideline
Toggle Quick Game Mode when you need less clutter and faster one-tap logging during a real game.
4
Tap the shot chart
Select a player, pick Made/Miss/FT, then tap the court. Shots beyond the 3-point line count automatically as 3.
5
Save every game
Hit Save Game after each game. Saved games feed your season charts, player comparison, and coaching reports.
6
Works offline
GameIQ Elite is a PWA. Install it to your home screen for instant access and full offline functionality at the gym.
