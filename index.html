Terms & Privacy

Last updated 2025 · All data stored locally on your device only.

Terms of Service

GameIQ Elite is provided for personal coaching and statistical tracking. All data you enter is stored locally in your browser. We do not collect, transmit, or sell your data. The app is provided "as is" without warranty. We are not liable for data loss — use Export Backup regularly.

Privacy Policy

No personal data is sent to any server. No analytics, tracking, or cookies beyond localStorage are used. The only external network requests are Google Fonts and (when you use AI Coach) Anthropic's API. AI Coach sends anonymized game context — aggregate stats only, never player names. Your Anthropic API key is stored in this browser's localStorage only and never transmitted to any server other than Anthropic's API. You can delete all data via Settings → Clear All Data at any time.

Children's Privacy

If tracking minors, use jersey numbers or initials rather than full names. Ensure you have appropriate consent from parents or guardians before tracking personal information about players under 18.
